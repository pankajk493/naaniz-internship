Butter Paneer Masala

Preparation Time: 20min

Servings: 4

Cost to prepare: 200

Categories: Bestsellers,Main Course

Mark Antony

DESCRIPTION

This recipe is an easy,quick and delicious method to make paneer butter masala.Within minutes you can prepare this Restaurant style paneer butter masala at home.

INGREDIENTS

Oil             100g

Onion              250g

Ginger            100g

Tomato           300g

Cashew/kaju           50g

Cream            100g

Paneer           400g

PROCEDURE

Firstly in a large kadai heat 1 tsp butter and 1 tsp oil.

Saute 1 onion, 1 inch ginger and 3 clove garlic until it shrinks slightly.

Now add 2 tomato and 10 cashew for a minute.

Cover and cook for 10 minutes or until tomatoes turn soft and mushy.

Cool completely and transfer to the blender.

Blend to smooth add past adding water if required.

In a kadai heat 2 tbsp butter and saute 2 pods cardamom and 1 bay leaf.
